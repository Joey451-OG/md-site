Why do I need to change my window size?

This website was designed for a specific aspect ratio (4:3). Additionally, most likely your current window resolution is 16:9. You may experience some spacing and other minor visual errors. Click the button bellow to open a new window with in the correct aspect ratio. To mobile users, this website is absolutely not designed for viewing on your device. Please switch to a more capable machine.
